Lemon Pepper Chicken

Sun 09 February 2020 By Jonathan Robey
Tags: Recipe

Tools:

  • Sous Vide setup
  • Quart freezer ziplock bags

Optional:

  • Parchment paper or aluminum foil as disposable workspace lining

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Fresh lemon
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Optional:

  • Tarragon
  • Italian herbs

Instructions:

  1. Cut lemon into thin slices
  2. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and other covering onto workspace (aluminum foil, plate, etc)
  3. Cover chicken breast with spices by patting onto dusted workspace
  4. Place chicken breast w/ 1 lemon slice into freezer ziplock and do preliminary evacuation of air
  5. Refrigerate/freeze until ready to cook
  6. Cook sous vide at 140 deg F for 1hr 30min
  7. Serve
