Rice Pilaf

Sun 09 February 2020 By Jonathan Robey
Tags: Recipe

Tools:

  • Instant pot pressure cooker

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (90g) orzo
  • 1 cup (180g) rice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp peanut oil
  • 3/2 cup (360g) water
  • 1 cup (240g) broth (chicken stock)

Instructions:

Using an instant pot:

  1. Saute orzo and rice in oil until begin to brown (~8min?)
  2. Cancel cook and mix in remaining ingredients
  3. Cook for 8 min on high
  4. Stand for 10 min, release pressure.
  5. Open, fluff, and serve

Comments:

This recipe came out reasonably well, but could likely be adjusted for better taste, perhaps including some other herbs.
